# Dream Detective 梦境侦探
02/2026

> GGJ China 2026 Game Project

[GGJ Webpage](https://globalgamejam.org/games/2026/mengjingzhentan-0) | [Play on itch.io](https://mambojiang.itch.io/dream-detective)

《梦境侦探》是一款2D剧情解谜游戏，你将成为一位拥有特殊能力的侦探，走进生者与死者的“梦境”。你将戴上面具，伪装成他们熟悉的人（或是小猫！），在人们的潜意识中寻找被刻意隐藏的线索。  
随着调查的深入，你竟发现，这起诡异的案件也与你的身份息息相关……  
面具之下，你能找到真相吗？

Dream Detective is a 2D narrative puzzle game. You take on the role of a detective with special abilities, stepping into the “dreams” of both the living and the dead. By wearing masks, you disguise yourself as someone familiar to them (or even a little cat!) and search within their subconscious for clues deliberately hidden away.

As the investigation deepens, you uncover that this strange case is closely tied to your own identity… Behind the mask, can you discover the truth?
