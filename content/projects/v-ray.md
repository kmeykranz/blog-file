# V-Ray
10/2022

> Finalist Team in 2022-2023 Conrad Challenge China

- Designed and developed V-Ray, a solar-powered 3D vision education device aimed at enhancing interactive learning experiences.
- Selected as a finalist in the Conrad Challenge China for innovative application of renewable energy and 3D visualization in education.
- Collaborated in a multidisciplinary team to conceptualize, prototype, and present the device to an international panel of judges.
